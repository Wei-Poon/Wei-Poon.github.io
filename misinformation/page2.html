Nairo

The
Veteran

Nairoby "Nairo" Quezada, born on November 7th, 1996, is a top competitor in the Brawl, Smash 4 and Ultimate eras. Based in Passaic, New Jersey, Nairo initially gained recognition throughout the community following his impressive showing at Apex 2012, eliminating players such as Yuta "Nietono" Uejima. In the preceding years, Nairo would slowly work his way up to be amongst the best of Brawl. Many considered him the best Brawl player at the end of the game's lifespan.

With the release of Super Smash Bros. for Wii U, Nairo remained a top player, frequently placing high at premier tournaments. A year after the game's release, Nairo made history as the only player to defeat ZeRo in 2015, ending the latter's 56 tournament win streak.

In the transition into Smash Ultimate, Nairo continued to place high in early tournaments. Relegating his Smash 4 main, Zero Suit Samus, to secondary, Nairo picked up Palutena as his Ultimate main and quickly became renowned as the best Palutena player in the world.

Legacy

Team Liquid
2015-2016

Nairo signed with Team Liquid at the end of what would be his most dominant period of competitive Smash 4. A couple of months into the signing, Nairo would historically go on to defeat ZeRo at MLG World Finals.

NRG Esports
2016-2020

With his dominant Smash 4 tournament showings, Nairo signed with new sponsor NRG Esports a year after he was acquired by Team Liquid. Under the NRG Esports team, Nairo remained a top 5 player in Smash 4 and transitioned into being a premier Ultimate player.

Historical
Streak Ender

A year following Smash 4's release, Nairo would become the only person to beat ZeRo in 2015, ending ZeRo's record 56 tournament streak.

Top 5 Smash 4
Player in the World

Adopting a largely aggressive and fast playstyle, Nairo found a lot of success at the higher levels of Smash 4 competition. He pioneered the aggressive pressure strategy with his Zero Suit Samus play.

Smash Duality

Nairo

The
Victim?

On July 1st, 2020, the Smash community experienced a wave of sexual misconduct allegations regarding high profile members. One of the allegations that surfaced accused Nairo of initiating sexual interactions with a minor.

The allegation received quick support from many prominent community members. It would also come to light that Nairo resorted to bribery as a form of damage control.

Months following the inital allegation, however, Nairo made an official statement refuting the original claims. Having collected a 30-page document with evidence, Nairo planned to take legal action and began receiving support from the community.

Timeline

Initial Allegation

Former player CaptainZack shares his account of a previous sexual encounter with Nairo, while he was still underaged.

Nairo issues an apology to CaptainZack for his behaviour and explains that he will be stepping away from the community to self-reflect and seek help.

Samsora's Statement
September 15th, 2020

Samsora, a player familiar with both CaptainZack and Nairo, releases his official statement discussing Zack's intention of speaking the truth rather than damaging Nairo's reputation.

Nairo's Second Response
October 29th, 2020

Following a brief hiatus, Nairo returns to make a statement with evidence regarding Zack's allegation as false and that he would be taking legal action. The statement recieves support from other prominent players.
